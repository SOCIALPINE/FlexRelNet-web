Problem

Why Current Medical AI Doesn't Reach

Model Size Barrier

ResNet-50: 97MB. VGG-16: 500MB+. These sizes prevent deployment on low-cost edge devices that rural clinics can afford.

Infrastructure Requirements

GPU servers, stable electricity, high-bandwidth internet, technical staff. Solar-powered clinics with intermittent connectivity are excluded.

Diagnostic Access Gap

Nearly half the global population lacks access to basic diagnostics. Radiologist ratio below 1:100,000 in many low-income countries.

Lancet Commission on Diagnostics, 2021
